About Church

This church is unique to be the first and only church in Malankara Orthodox church to be dedicated in the name of St. Joseph. St. Joseph is remembered as the righteous in the Orthodox church. God chose him for a significant role in His salvation plan. He was chosen to be the husband of Mother Mary, to protect Mother Mary and child Jesus. St. Joseph is known as the patron saint of workers, homeless and family. St. Joseph, may your prayers bless this humble church to spread God's love!
