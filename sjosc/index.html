About Church

St. Joseph Orthodox church is a parish of Bangalore Diocese of the Malankara Orthodox Syrian Church. This is the first and only Malankara Orthodox church dedicated in the name of St. Joseph.

This church is an offshoot of the St. Thomas Orthodox Maha Edavaka, Bangalore East. The parish has 120 members with Rev Fr.Binoy Joshua as the vicar.
